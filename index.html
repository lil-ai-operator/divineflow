Session Preparation Guide

Preparing for your distance healing session is simple — it's mostly about creating space for yourself.

Before Your Session

  • Find a quiet, comfortable space where you won't be disturbed for the full duration
  • Wear loose, comfortable clothing
  • Have a glass of water nearby
  • Turn your phone to silent (or airplane mode)
  • Let Clare know if you have any specific concerns or areas you'd like to focus on

During Your Session

  • You'll receive a video call link (Zoom, WhatsApp, or phone call)
  • There's no need to have your camera on — you can simply lie down or sit comfortably with your eyes closed
  • Clare will guide you through a brief check-in, then the healing work begins
  • Simply breathe, relax, and allow whatever arises to arise
  • You might feel warmth, tingling, emotional releases, or deep stillness — all are welcome

After Your Session

  • Take a moment to gently come back — stretch, sip water
  • You may feel deeply relaxed or surprisingly energized
  • Some people experience immediate shifts; others notice changes over the following days
  • Feel free to journal any thoughts, feelings, or insights that come up
  • Drink plenty of water and be kind to yourself

Common Questions

Does distance healing actually work?

Yes — many clients experience real and meaningful shifts from distance healing. Energy isn't limited by physical location, and countless people have found healing through remote sessions. The key is being open to the possibility.

Do I need to believe in healing for it to work?

Not at all. Some of our most skeptical clients have been pleasantly surprised. While openness helps, you don't need faith — just a willingness to try.

What if I can't feel anything during the session?

That's completely normal and perfectly fine. Healing doesn't always produce obvious sensations. Some people feel a lot; others feel nothing tangible but still notice changes afterward. Both are valid.

How many sessions will I need?

It depends on what you're working with. Some people feel relief after one session; others prefer ongoing support. Clare will help you figure out what's right for you during your first session.

Can I do distance healing alongside other treatments?

Absolutely. Distance healing complements other approaches — it's not an alternative to medical care. Many clients continue working with doctors, therapists, or other practitioners alongside healing sessions.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

Life happens. Just give at least 24 hours' notice if you need to reschedule, and we'll find another time that works. Late cancellations may incur a small fee.

Is this religious?

No. DivineFlow is a secular practice rooted in energy healing traditions. You don't need to follow any particular belief system — it's about restoring balance to your energy, whatever that means to you.

How do I know if distance healing is right for me?

If you're curious, open, or feeling called to try something new for your wellbeing — that's enough. There's no commitment. Book a first session and see how it feels. You can always decide it's not for you, but you won't know until you try.

Booking FAQ

How does distance healing work?

Distance healing works through focused intention and energy connection. It doesn't require physical presence — we can be on opposite sides of the world and the healing energy still flows. You'll find a quiet spot, relax, and I'll channel healing energy to you remotely.

What if I've never done anything like this before?

That's completely fine! Many of my clients are new to healing work. Just come with an open mind and willingness to relax. I'll guide you through everything.

How do I book a session?

Simply click the "Book Now" button on any pricing card. You'll be taken to my calendar to choose a time that works for you. Payment is taken at booking.

What happens after I book?

You'll receive a confirmation email with details of what to expect. Before our session, I'll send you a brief questionnaire to help me understand what you'd like to focus on.

Can I reschedule or cancel?

Yes! Life happens. You can reschedule or cancel up to 24 hours before your session for a full refund. Less than 24 hours notice, the session is non-refundable but can be rescheduled.

What should I do during the session?

Find a comfortable, quiet space where you won't be disturbed. Lie down or sit comfortably. You can close your eyes, focus on your breath, or simply allow yourself to relax. There's no specific position required.
