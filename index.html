About Clare

I work with people who feel stuck, overwhelmed, or out of balance — whether that's stress, emotional blocks, physical discomfort, or just a sense that something needs to shift. My approach is gentle, intuitive, and completely tailored to you.

What Is Distance Healing?

Distance healing — sometimes called remote healing or absent healing — is a practice where healing energy is sent across space using focused intention and ancient techniques.

1

We book a session at a time that suits you

2

You find a quiet, comfortable spot wherever you are

3

I connect with you remotely and channel healing energy

4

You simply relax, breathe, and allow the healing to flow

You might feel warmth, tingling, deep relaxation, or sometimes nothing at all — and that's completely okay. Healing works in its own time and way.
